If you have a "Yes" in the MTK column you should first download newest MTKFLASH from the MT xxxx firmwares part and run readout.bat to make a backup of your existing firmware.
These drives are in the most cases also after a bad flash recoverable.

First upgrade your drive with the official firmware upgrade from Samsung. (If this fail you can contact the support)
Than you can flash the patched version in the same way like the official upgrade. (Remove the .rpc1 extension before you flash) If the official Firmware is newer please mail me

Please mail me if you use an as untested marked firmware.
