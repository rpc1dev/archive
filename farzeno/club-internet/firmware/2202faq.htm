SD-C2202 1R17 MiniFAQ

 

1.(Most common problem) After the flash, i can't boot or/and the Bios give me an IDE error.

After the flash the drive became a master drive even if it was in slave position . There is no jumper on the DVD drive, the master/slave position is stored in the firmware. 1R17 is a master firmware. After the flash you have 2 primary master drive (the HDD and the DVD) it's why the notebook won't start or give you an error.
Solution : You have to change manually the position of the HHD to slave position. That should solve the problem.

2. After the flash i can't check the DMA option? DVD playing is choppy.

This problem occur generally on notebook who have an ALI chipset (like the IBM Thinkpad I-serie 1400/1500 and probably other).
Solution : You have to download the latest ALI IDE drivers version 3.50 or 3.55 at www.ali.com.tw

3. When trying to play DVD-video, nothing happen?

Solution See second question. In some case, it could be usefull to uninstall the DVD software and reinstall it.

4. My drive is now RPC-1 but i always have a limited number of region change allowed / or i can't play other region DVD?

Solution : Hell, even if the drive is RPC-1 you have to hack your DVDsoftware. You should know that!! Look on www.dvdutils.com to see how to hack your DVD software. If there is no hack for your software, you have to find another DVD good player easily hackable, like PowerDVD or Cinemaster99.
