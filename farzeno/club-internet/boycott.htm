DVD drives to boycott!!

It's time to launch a big boycott to all RPC-2 drives (the drives who are region locked in firmware).

What you have to do :
-First, don't buy any of these drives.
-Return them to your resellers if you got one of them.
-Send tons of email to the companies who manufacture these drives.
I think the only way to make them changing position, is an economical pressure until they release a region free firmware (RPC1), like they do before.
After all RPC2 drive have no reason to exist since MPEG2/DVD software are already region protected.

These drives are region protected in firmware without solution actually to make them region free.
If you already have one of these drives, try to return it to your resellers and buy a region free one.
If you can't, send tons of e-mail to manufacturers to provide a region free firmware.
